About Us

JamhuriCan Investments Inc is an investment group formed by a collective of like-minded individual members who share a common interest in achieving financial independence through pooled investments. It is a legal entity federally registered with the Government of Canada and domiciled in Toronto, Canada. JamhuriCan Investments Inc has an operational bank account where members deposit money to purchase shares for pooled investments as stipulated in the Constitution. The day to day operations and running of the investment company is by a Board of Directors duly elected by its members.

Our mission

To operate within the legal framework of law in jurisdictions of operation.

To engage in investments and business operations that are ethical and in compliance with necessary regulations.

To establish relationships and measures which promote the development of the individual members and the group.

Establish due diligence, mechanisms, and processes which protect the financial interests of all its members.

To engage in investment activities both in Canada and Kenya for the sole purpose of the promotion and development of individual members as governed by their Constitution.
